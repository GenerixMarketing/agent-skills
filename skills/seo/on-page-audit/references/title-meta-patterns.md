# Title and Meta Description Patterns

Templates for the most common page types. Use them as starting points, not formulas. Always read `client-context.md` before applying so the brand voice carries through.

Replace bracketed placeholders with real values. Aim for 50-60 characters on titles, 140-160 on meta descriptions.

## Service Page (Local)

**Title patterns:**
- `[Service] in [City] | [Brand]`
- `[City] [Service], Free Estimate | [Brand]`
- `[Brand] | [Service] for [City] Homeowners`

**Meta patterns:**
- `[Brand] provides [service] across [city/region]. [One-sentence value prop]. Get a free estimate today.`
- `Looking for [service] in [city]? [Brand] has served [region] for [N] years. [One short proof point]. Call or request an estimate.`

**Example:**
- Title: `Roof Replacement in Indianapolis | Cardinal Ridge Roofing` (55 chars)
- Meta: `Cardinal Ridge has replaced over 2,000 roofs across the Indianapolis metro since 2003. Insurance claim help included. Get a free estimate today.` (149 chars)

## Service Page (National or Online)

**Title patterns:**
- `[Service] | [Brand]`
- `[Brand], [Service] for [Audience]`
- `[Outcome] with [Service] | [Brand]`

**Meta patterns:**
- `[Brand] helps [audience] [achieve outcome] through [service]. [One short proof point]. See how it works.`

## Product Page (Ecommerce)

**Title patterns:**
- `[Product Name], [Key Attribute] | [Brand]`
- `[Product Name] | [Brand]`
- `Buy [Product Name] [Modifier] | [Brand]`

**Meta patterns:**
- `[Product name] [key attributes]. [Benefit]. Free shipping over $[X]. [Brand].`
- `Shop [product name] in [color/size/material options]. [One-sentence value prop]. Order today.`

## Blog Post (Informational)

**Title patterns:**
- `[Topic]: A Complete Guide`
- `How to [Outcome] (Without [Common Pain])`
- `[Number] Ways to [Outcome] in [Year]`
- `What Is [Concept]? [Plain Language Definition]`

**Meta patterns:**
- `[One-sentence promise of what the reader will learn]. Includes [proof of depth: examples, data, templates, etc.].`
- `Wondering [implicit question]? This guide covers [3-4 specific subtopics] so you can [outcome].`

**Example:**
- Title: `How to Choose a Roofing Contractor (Without Getting Burned)` (59 chars)
- Meta: `Hiring the wrong roofer costs more than just money. This guide covers the 7 questions every homeowner should ask before signing a contract.` (140 chars)

## Location Landing Page

**Title patterns:**
- `[Service] in [Neighborhood], [City] | [Brand]`
- `[Brand] [Service] | Serving [Neighborhood]`

**Meta patterns:**
- `[Brand] serves [neighborhood] and the surrounding [city] area with [service]. [One specific local proof point: nearby projects, response time, local references].`

Avoid: generic templates that swap only the city name. Google's helpful content system penalizes these.

## FAQ Page

**Title patterns:**
- `[Topic] FAQ | [Brand]`
- `Frequently Asked Questions, [Topic] | [Brand]`
- `[Topic]: Common Questions Answered | [Brand]`

**Meta patterns:**
- `Answers to the most common questions about [topic]: [3 sample questions, lightly paraphrased]. From [Brand].`

## About / Company Page

**Title patterns:**
- `About [Brand] | [One-Line Value Prop]`
- `Meet the Team at [Brand]`

**Meta patterns:**
- `[Brand] has been [doing what] since [year]. Founded by [founder] to [purpose]. Learn about our team and approach.`

## Comparison Page

**Title patterns:**
- `[Option A] vs. [Option B]: Which Is Right for You?`
- `[Option A] vs. [Option B], A Side-by-Side Comparison`

**Meta patterns:**
- `Choosing between [A] and [B]? This guide compares them on [3 dimensions] so you can make the right call for [audience].`

## Patterns to Avoid

- **Pipe-delimited keyword stuffing.** `Roofing | Indianapolis | Roof Repair | Replacement | Cardinal Ridge` reads like spam and gets penalized.
- **Vague placeholders.** "Home" as a title. "Welcome to our website" as a meta. Both waste the impression.
- **All caps anywhere.** Reads as shouting and rarely gets clicked.
- **Year-in-title trap.** "Best Roofers 2024" goes stale fast. Use "2026" or drop the year entirely.
- **Brand name first on every page.** Save brand for the end or omit on long pages. The query keyword should lead.
- **Meta descriptions that just repeat the title.** Use the 140-160 characters to add information, not echo it.
