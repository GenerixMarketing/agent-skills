**Tools used:** Ahrefs Site Explorer 2026-05-22 (Cardinal Ridge plus three direct competitors), GSC Links report, manual brand search across major publications, citation audit via BrightLocal free scan

- **Referring domains:** 47
- **Domain rating estimate:** 18 (Ahrefs DR)

Cardinal Ridge has BBB only. Competitor B has been featured in two local home-and-garden magazines.

## Top Five Off-Page Priorities

1. **Apply to Indianapolis Builders Association and obtain member directory listing.** Workstream: Citations / Industry Associations. ~3 hours total including application paperwork. Realistic outcome: 1 high-quality industry-relevant dofollow link with sustained presence. Timeline: 4-8 weeks for membership review. Owner: business owner (signs application).
2. **Build and pitch original-data piece on Indianapolis-area storm damage trends.** Workstream: Digital PR / Content-Earned Links. ~16 hours total (8 to compile data from public sources and Cardinal Ridge's own job records, 8 to write and pitch). Realistic outcome: 3-8 placements across local TV news web pages, the Indianapolis Star, and regional home publications. Timeline: 60-90 days from start to first placements. Owner: writer + business owner for data input.
3. **Establish expert sourcing presence on Connectively and Qwoted.** Workstream: Digital PR. ~30 minutes daily for monitoring, ~1 hour weekly for pitch responses. Realistic outcome: 2-6 placements per month within 60 days. Timeline: starts immediately, results compound over 6 months. Owner: business owner or designated employee with subject expertise (NOT outsourced ghostwriter; reporters need genuine source).
4. **Claim and complete the 12 unclaimed or incomplete Tier 1 citations.** Workstream: Local Citations. ~4 hours total. Specifically: complete Apple Maps Business Connect (currently auto-populated), claim Bing Places, claim Foursquare, finish Nextdoor for Business profile, claim Yellow Pages, optimize BBB profile, update Yelp categories. Realistic outcome: 12 NAP-consistent listings completed. Quick win, foundational. Timeline: 2 weeks. Owner: marketing assistant or owner.
5. **Reactivate two recoverable lost links.** Workstream: Reclamation. ~30 minutes per outreach. Realistic outcome: 1-2 reinstated links. Timeline: 1-2 weeks. Owner: any account manager with email access.

## Active Workstreams

### 1. Digital PR and Expert Sourcing

- **Platforms to monitor:** Connectively (primary, daily), Qwoted (secondary, daily), Featured (weekly check)
- **Topic categories to respond to:** roofing (any), insurance claims for roof damage, storm preparedness, home maintenance for Indiana climate, hail damage assessment, choosing a contractor (red flags), seasonal home maintenance
- **Pitch templates:** See `references/outreach-templates.md` (Expert Sourcing Response)
- **Cadence:** 15 minutes daily for monitoring, batch responses on Tuesday and Thursday afternoons
- **Owner:** Business owner. Voice and credentials need to be his. Designated employee can monitor and flag opportunities, but quotes need to come from the owner.

Pitch volume target: 8-12 responses per week. Acceptance rate for new sources is typically 10-20% after a 60-day ramp.

### 2. Local Citations and Industry Associations

- **Foundational citation completions:** 12 citations to complete or repair (see Priority 4 above). Specifically:

  - Apple Business Connect (auto-populated, needs manual claim and completion)
  - Bing Places for Business (not claimed)
  - Yelp (claimed, categories incomplete)
  - Facebook Page Business profile (active, hours not synced)
  - Foursquare (not claimed)
  - Yellow Pages (not claimed)
  - Better Business Bureau (claimed, photos and services list outdated)
  - Nextdoor for Business (not claimed)
  - Angi (paying for leads, not optimized as listed provider)

Specific orgs: Indianapolis Habitat for Humanity (relevant cause, real link earner), Riley Hospital fundraiser (local goodwill, link earner), local high school athletic sponsorship (small but durable).
- **Local press pitching:** Indianapolis Star home section (quarterly pitches), WTHR storm-season expert availability (proactive outreach before storm season starts), Indianapolis Monthly home issue (annual feature pitch).

### 3. Partner and Vendor Links

- **Vendor directories to apply to:** GAF MasterElite contractor program (apply for upgrade from current Certified status; MasterElite earns higher placement in GAF contractor locator). CertainTeed SELECT ShingleMaster credential (apply, earns directory listing).
- **Partner backlink opportunities:**
  - Insurance carriers Cardinal Ridge works with on claims. Three are on the State Farm preferred-contractor list (already linking). Apply to Allstate, Liberty Mutual, and Travelers preferred-contractor programs. Each lists current preferred contractors on their consumer-facing pages.
  - Two local supply houses Cardinal Ridge buys from regularly. Both have "contractors we work with" pages. Request listing.
  - One referring partner (local home inspector who refers roof inspections). Mutual link swap on contact pages.
- **Testimonial trade opportunities:** GAF customer testimonial program. Submit a genuine testimonial as a contractor; GAF uses on their pro site with link credit.

### 4. Content-Earned Links

- **Cornerstone resource plan:** Original-data storm damage report (see Priority 2). Compiled from NOAA storm data for the metro plus Cardinal Ridge's own job records (anonymized). Frames as "what we saw this year in Indianapolis-area roof damage." Pitchable angles: insurance trends, neighborhood damage patterns, seasonal timing. Target 4-8 local press placements.
- **Useful tool to consider:** Simple "is it time to replace your roof" interactive checklist embedded on the site. Easy to build, easy to share, earns links from any "should I replace my roof" content piece. ~12 hours of dev time. Defer to Q2 unless prioritized.
- **Year-in-review piece (annual):** "2026 in roofing" recap published December: storm events, material price trends, code changes in Indiana. Published in December earns links throughout the following year. ~10 hours of writer time, ~2 of owner SME input.

### 5. Reclamation and Maintenance

- **Unlinked mentions identified:** 7 unlinked brand mentions found via manual search. Six are on local home and lifestyle blogs; one is on the Indianapolis Star website (the easiest valuable target). Outreach using the "Unlinked Brand Mention Reclamation" template in references.
- **Broken inbound links:** 3 inbound links pointing to URLs that 404 on Cardinal Ridge's site. Two are recoverable via 301 redirect (URLs were renamed in the 2024 restructure; redirects need to be added per `technical-audit` Top Five Fix #1). One points to a deleted blog post; either restore the post or 301 to the most relevant live page.
- **Lost-link recovery opportunities:** Two recoverable lost links per the current-state assessment. Both are local home-and-garden blogs that pruned old content. Polite outreach asking if they would be open to reinstating in a relevant section.
