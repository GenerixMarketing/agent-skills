**Tools used:** GSC export 2026-05-22 (top 200 queries, last 90 days), Semrush keyword research and competitor pull, Google autocomplete, AlsoAsked, manual SERP inspection on top 30 candidates
**Scope:** Residential roof replacement service area (Indianapolis metro, Marion/Hamilton/Hendricks/Johnson counties)

## Executive Summary

Cardinal Ridge has clear, winnable demand across both core service terms ("roof replacement [neighborhood]") and storm-season transactional terms ("storm damage roof inspection"). The biggest unaddressed opportunity is the neighborhood-specific service tier: each of the four major metro counties has 8-15 winnable transactional terms the site currently does not rank for. A secondary opportunity sits in commercial investigation terms ("roof replacement cost," "asphalt vs metal roof") where one well-built cost guide could anchor a topic cluster and feed the existing service pages. Recommended sequencing: ship the neighborhood pages first (highest direct-revenue impact), then the cost guide cluster.

## Themes and Patterns

The four major themes that emerged from this research:

1. **Neighborhood-specific transactional terms are the highest-leverage workstream.** Each of the four metro counties has 8-15 winnable terms the site does not currently rank for. Combined volume across all county neighborhoods is roughly 1,800-2,200 monthly searches, with average KD under 25. Estimated 12-18 closeable jobs per year from this cluster alone, at Cardinal Ridge's typical close rate.
2. **Insurance and storm damage is an underexploited authority area.** Cardinal Ridge has 20+ years of insurance claim experience but no content reflecting it. Competitors lean on "we handle insurance" as a one-line bullet. A 5-7 page cluster covering claim filing, common denials, what insurance covers, and the actual process would be defensible category authority.
3. **Cost transparency is a content gap across the local market.** None of the top three direct competitors have a substantive cost page. A single well-built cost guide could rank for a half-dozen high-intent commercial keywords and become the top internal-linking anchor for the service pages.
4. **Year-in-keyword terms ("roof replacement cost 2026") show meaningful volume but decay fast.** Worth one page that gets refreshed annually rather than a new page per year.

2. **Sprints 2-4 (next 6 weeks):** Rebuild the four core neighborhood pages (Carmel, Fishers, Greenwood, Westfield). Each takes 6 hours of writer time per the on-page audit estimate, plus owner review at 48-72 hour turnaround. Net 24-30 writer hours plus review cycles.

4. **Quarter 2:** Build the insurance/storm cluster. 5-7 cornerstone pages. Higher writer effort because the topics require client SME input.
5. **Quarter 3:** Build the cost guide and materials cluster. 3-4 cornerstone pages.
6. **Ongoing:** Quarterly GSC pull to monitor Tier 2 and Tier 3 terms for movement. Promote to Tier 1 as data warrants.
